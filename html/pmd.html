BPM Core

Withings BPM Core is a personal medical device that combines a traditional blood pressure monitor with additional health tracking capabilities. It measures blood pressure, detects irregular heart rhythms, and provides a 3-lead ECG recording. The device syncs with the Withings Health Mate app, allowing users to track their readings over time and share them with healthcare professionals if needed.
